# Session Log — 2026-06-27

## What was done
Settled the proposal structure. Confirmed 10% is inside the
self-approvable discount band; product confirmed bulk-import is in
discovery with no committable date. Worked through the three candidate
structures against those two facts.

## Decisions made and why
- **10% discount tied to a 2-year commit.** Full reasoning captured in
  [[02-context/decisions/2026-06-27-acme-pricing-discount-structure|the decision note]]
  — short version: the 2-year term buys runway to close the bulk-import
  gap and moves Acme past procurement's next vendor re-evaluation,
  while 10% avoids finance escalation.
- **Bulk-import referenced as roadmap intent only, not a contractual
  deliverable.** Why: product won't commit a date, and promising one
  contractually converts a product gap into a legal liability.
